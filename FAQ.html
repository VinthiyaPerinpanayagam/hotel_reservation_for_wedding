FAQ

  • What kind of events can the banquet halls be rented for?

    Our Banquet Halls are ideal for a variety of events, including Anniversary Celebrations, Wedding Receptions, Corporate Meetings and workshops, Christening Celebrations, Reunions, Staff Christmas Parties, Art Exhibitions, Birthday Parties, Seminars, Auctions, Trade shows, and more.

  • Is parking free?

    Yes, we are the only location within the vicinity that can accommodate up-to 1000 vehicles within the premises.

  • Can we bring our own food?

    Foods prepared from outside are not allowed due to food safety and liability issues.

  • May I bring in my own Decorator?

    Yes, you can personalize your decor for the wedding venue by hiring someone or decorating yourself.

  • Do you provide bar services?

    Yes, we do. You can speak to our banquet sales department with regards to your requirements and they will customize a package and service element for you.

  • How is the seating arranged?

    The banquet operations division will arrange or suggest an appropriate seating configuration based on the number of guests. A floor plan is provided, and the client decides on the location of the head table and guest tables. The WE staff will set up the tables and chairs accordingly.
